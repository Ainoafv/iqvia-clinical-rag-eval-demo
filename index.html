Overview · 2 / 4

The principle that drives everything

"I treat AI outputs as hypotheses to validate — not results to trust."

The model proposes. An evaluation harness checks retrieval quality, groundedness, and policy rules. A qualified human approves, edits, or rejects. Nothing ships on model confidence alone.

This mirrors production patterns from regulated healthcare AI: evaluation gates before release, claim-level grounding checks, structured JSON output, and append-only audit trails.

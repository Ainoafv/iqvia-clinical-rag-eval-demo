Design principle

I treat AI outputs as hypotheses to validate — not results to trust.

The model proposes. The grounding engine and evaluation harness decide. A qualified reviewer approves. Styled after enterprise life-sciences UI patterns (IQVIA Apollo: blue surfaces, green accents, gauge-style metrics).
